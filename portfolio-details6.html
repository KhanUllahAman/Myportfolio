I have developed a mobile app called "Laptop Harbor E-commerce," which is centered around selling affordable laptops. This project was my end-of-semester project for the 4th semester at Aptech. I utilized Flutter and Dart technologies, along with Firebase database. The app features functionalities such as Google sign-in, email verification, user interface enhancements, secure login, and many more. Feel free to download the APK and enjoy using the app.
